## The idea

Most investors overtrade because decisions feel cheap.
Buffett's 20-punch card rule forces you to treat each investment like it costs one slot of your lifetime.

This tool tracks your 20 slots — and makes you justify every one.

## Features

- Track up to 20 lifetime investment decisions
- Record your thesis for each
- Review past decisions and outcomes
- Free, no login, data stored locally
